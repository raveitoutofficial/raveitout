Mixtapes mode in RIO is the course mode.

Edit RIO_COURSE_FOLDERS in Scripts/01 SYSTEM_PARAMETERS.lua to add your own course folder.

Add a banner.png to your folder for the folder banner.

As for course tags:

COURSE: The name of the course
BANNER: The jacket of the course
SCRIPTER: Who made this course, a replacement for the song artist shown during gameplay and selection.
DESCRIPTION: If you want a description for your course. Used as a replacement for the text shown during the song information screen before gameplay.

Refer to https://github.com/stepmania/stepmania/wiki/Courses for other stuff.

If you are looking for information on Special Mode please refer to Scripts/09 QuestMode.lua for information on how to add new missions.
